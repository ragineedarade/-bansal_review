OUR FACILITIES

Bansal Group of Institution offers modern classrooms, sports facilities, a hygienic canteen, and a vibrant campus environment.

CANTEEN

Bansal Group of Institution's canteen offers a hygienic and comfortable environment for students and staff.
Serving a variety of delicious and affordable meals, it's the perfect spot to relax and enjoy refreshments between classes.

LARGEST PLAYGROUND

The sports facilities promote physical fitness and team spirit. Students can engage in cricket, football, basketball, and more, with modern equipment and well-maintained grounds.

CLASS ROOMS

Spacious, modern classrooms equipped with smart boards and advanced teaching tools, providing an ideal learning environment that blends traditional and digital education.

LIBRARY

The library offers a vast collection of books, research papers, and digital resources. It's a peaceful space where students can focus on their studies and research, enhancing their academic journey.

LABORATORIES

Equipped with state-of-the-art tools, our laboratories provide practical experience in various fields, helping students gain hands-on skills that complement their theoretical knowledge.

TRANSPORTATION

BGI College is pleased to offer convenient transportation services to ensure a smooth and accessible commute for our students. Our college bus service operates on a well-planned schedule.
