About Us

We Are India’s Best Engineering College

The Bansal Group of Institutes (BGI) stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Central India, committed to providing exceptional learning opportunities since its establishment in 2000. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences they need to thrive in today’s fast-paced and ever-evolving world.

At BGI, we pride ourselves on our focus on academic excellence and practical skill-building, making us a premier destination for students seeking a comprehensive education. Our dedicated team of experienced and highly qualified professionals fosters a supportive learning environment, ensuring that every student can achieve their full potential.

We offer a diverse range of programs, including undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and management, tailored to meet the demands of the industry.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, and a well-stocked library, all designed to enhance the learning experience. However, our commitment to student development extends beyond academics. We emphasize holistic growth through a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and community service initiatives.

At the Bansal Group of Institutes, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible and ready to make a positive impact in the world.
